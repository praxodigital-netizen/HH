# Hemant Healthcare Private Limited – Website

A modern, professional hospital website for **Hemant Healthcare Private Limited**, Roorkee, Uttarakhand.

## Project Overview

- **Type**: Single-page React website
- **Stack**: React 18 + TypeScript + Vite 7 + Tailwind CSS
- **Purpose**: Showcase hospital online presence with premium design

## Design System

- **Fonts**: Playfair Display (display/headings) + DM Sans (body)
- **Color Palette**: Medical blue (#0369a1), accent sky (#0ea5e9), white background, light grey sections
- **Style**: Clean, modern healthcare, professional, patient-friendly
- **Layout**: Single-page with smooth scroll navigation

## Sections

1. **Navbar** – Fixed top with logo, transparent → white on scroll, mobile responsive, top info bar with phone & email
2. **Hero** – Text-focused centered layout with subtle hospital exterior background (very low opacity, blurred, white overlays), soft gradient with decorative orbs, badge + heading + subheading + CTAs + trust highlights, stats strip below with smooth connection
3. **About** – Split layout (reception image + content), highlight cards, "20+ Years" badge
4. **History** – Reverse split layout (content + consultation room image), Est. 2004 card
5. **Vision & Mission** – Dark gradient background, two glass-morph cards with Eye & Target icons
6. **Services** – Surgical departments (5 cards) + Diagnostics (6 items: MRI, CT, Ultrasound, X-Ray, Mammography, OPG) + OT photo gallery
7. **Doctors** – 6 doctor cards (Dr. Hemant Gupta, Dr. Kokila Gupta, Dr. Anurag Garg, Dr. Deepak Semwal, Dr. Rachit Ahuja, Dr. Sounak Chabri) with photos/initials
8. **Facilities** – 6 facility cards (HDU, Indoor Patient, Ambulance, MRI & CT Scan, Pharmacy, Canteen) with images
9. **Why Choose Us** – Dark gradient background, 6 trust cards with stats
10. **Certifications** – Registration certificate section with View/Download PDF buttons
11. **Contact** – 3 contact cards (Phone, Email, Location), prominent Call Now button
12. **Footer** – Logo, quick links, services, contact info, Praxo Digital credit

## Key Features

- Hospital logo in header and footer
- Intersection Observer scroll animations
- Animated count-up counters
- Responsive design (mobile-first)
- "Call Now" and "Contact Us" CTAs (no booking system)
- Smooth scroll navigation
- Back to Top button
- Registration certificate PDF download
- Real hospital/doctor photographs
- Doctor initials for those without photos
